# 2H Goal Model - Prediction Tracker

## What Is This?

This is a **data-driven prediction model** that analyzes soccer matches at half-time and calculates the **probability that the home team will score in the second half**.

All predictions are posted **live during the half-time break** based on:
- First-half performance data
- Teams' 2H scoring patterns
- Historical statistics
- Match context and momentum

Every prediction is tracked publicly with full transparency.

---

## How to Read the Stats

### Total Picks
The number of predictions that have been completed (won or lost). Pending predictions don't count until the match finishes.

### Win Rate
Percentage of predictions that were correct.

**Example:** 15 wins out of 20 completed picks = 75% win rate

### Net Correct
Shows how many more predictions were correct than incorrect.

**Formula:** Correct predictions - Incorrect predictions

**Examples:**
- 10 correct, 4 incorrect = **+6** net correct
- 15 correct, 15 incorrect = **0** net correct  
- 8 correct, 12 incorrect = **-4** net correct

A positive number means the model is performing well.

## How Predictions Work

### Prediction Format

Each prediction includes:

**Match:** Team A vs Team B (at half-time)  
**Prediction:** Home Team 2H Over 0.5 Goals (home team will score at least once in second half)  
**Probability:** 73% (model's confidence that home team scores in 2H)  
**Analysis:** Key factors the model identified

### Understanding Probabilities

The model calculates the likelihood that the **home team scores in the second half**:

- **70%+ probability** = High confidence home team scores
- **40-70% probability** = Moderate confidence
- **Below 40% probability** = Model predicts home team likely won't score

### Example Predictions

**High confidence:**
```
Leverkusen vs Wolfsburg (at HT)
Prediction: Home Team 2H Over 0.5 Goals
Probability: 93%
```
The model is very confident Leverkusen will score in 2H.

**Lower confidence:**
```
Heidenheim vs M'Gladbach (at HT)  
Prediction: Home Team 2H Over 0.5 Goals
Probability: 45%
```
The model thinks it's low probability Heidenheim scores in 2H.

### When Predictions Are Posted

**During half-time only** - never before the match starts

Why? The model uses first-half data to make accurate predictions about the second half.

### Result Tracking

After each match ends:
- **WIN** = Home team scored in 2H (prediction correct)
- **LOSS** = Home team didn't score in 2H (prediction incorrect)
- The final score is shown with 2H breakdown

## Understanding the Model

### What It Predicts

**One specific thing:** Will the home team score at least once in the second half?

The model outputs a probability percentage:
- **High probability (70%+)** = Home team likely to score in 2H
- **Medium probability (40-70%)** = Moderate chance
- **Low probability (below 40%)** = Home team unlikely to score in 2H

### What Makes This Different

**NOT based on:**
- Gut feelings
- "Hot tips"
- Random guesses
- Pre-match predictions

**Based on:**
- Statistical analysis of first-half performance
- Home team's historical 2H scoring patterns
- Current match state and momentum
- Data-driven probability calculation

### The Half-Time Advantage

Analyzing at half-time provides crucial information:
- How the home team performed in first 45 minutes
- Current score and match dynamics
- Momentum and tactical adjustments needed
- Fatigue levels and substitution patterns

The model processes this live data to calculate 2H scoring probability.

### Focus on Home Teams

Why only home teams?
- Home advantage creates more consistent patterns
- Better data quality for home team behavior
- More predictable 2H performance
- Clearer model accuracy measurement
