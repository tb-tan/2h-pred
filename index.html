About This Model

What is this?

A data-driven model that predicts whether the home team will score in the second half. All predictions are posted live during half-time with a probability percentage based on first-half data and statistical analysis.

How to read probabilities

  • 70%+ = High confidence home team scores in 2H
  • 50-70% = Moderate confidence
  • Below 50% = Home team unlikely to score in 2H

Example: 93% probability means the model is very confident the home team will score. 45% means it's unlikely.

What does "Net Correct" mean?

Net Correct = Correct predictions minus incorrect predictions.

  • 10 correct, 4 incorrect = +6 net correct
  • 15 correct, 15 incorrect = 0 net correct
  • 8 correct, 12 incorrect = -4 net correct

A positive number means the model is performing well. Simple as that.

Full transparency

Every prediction is tracked publicly. Wins and losses. No cherry-picking. No hiding bad days. What you see is exactly what was posted.
